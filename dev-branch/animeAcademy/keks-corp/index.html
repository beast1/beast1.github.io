KEKS CORP believes in a peaceful world where people are free to live by their traditions and feel safe so they can live to the full extent of their dreams. KEKS CORP is the effective consultancy link between security/military equipment producer and Governments. In regards to procurement needs, we strive to offer only high- quality partner products and provide a variety of services for our customers all around the world. Our worldwide partner production and repair facilities have decades long tradition that reaches back to 1920s.
